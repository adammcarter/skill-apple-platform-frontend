# visionOS Notes

Use this reference for visionOS frontend work.

## Core Expectations

- Comfort, clarity, and spatial stability come first.
- Depth should communicate structure, not novelty for its own sake.
- Ornament placement and motion should support orientation and reduce cognitive load.

## Interaction

- Make gaze and gesture targets easy to perceive and select.
- Avoid rapid or unnecessary movement in peripheral content.
- Preserve continuity when content changes location or scale.

## Visual Style

- Keep hierarchy strong and scenes legible.
- Avoid excessive layered ornamentation that competes with content.
- Use motion to explain spatial relationships, not to show off the medium.
