# watchOS And tvOS Notes

Use this reference for watchOS or tvOS frontend work.

## watchOS

- Prioritize glanceability and immediacy.
- Keep content focused on one clear task or status at a time.
- Avoid cramped multi-control layouts.

## tvOS

- Design around focus movement and distance viewing.
- Make hierarchy legible from across the room.
- Motion should reinforce focus movement and selection without overwhelming the content.

## Shared Rule

- Design for the dominant input model first rather than porting another platform's layout mechanically.
